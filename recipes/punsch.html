Christmas in a Glass

It might not be the season yet, but let me tell you: it’s never too early to prepare for Christmas!

The Austrian and German equivalent of the US-American eggnogg is Punsch - a sweet and hot drink, made from tea, freshly pressed fruit juice, alcohol and a lot of spices.

Now, let's stop talking! Let's make some Punsch!

Ingredients

You need the following ingredients for 1 jar of Punsch:

  • 500 ml fruit tea
  • 500 ml freshly pressed orange juice
  • 200 ml dark rum
  • Sugar or honey
  • Spices: cinnamon, cloves, anise

Steps

The process of making Punsch is super easy:

  1. Put all the liquid ingredients - except the alcohol - into a big pot and heat them, but: make sure that they don't boil!
  2. Add as much sugar or honey as you like, to achieve your preferred level of sweetness.
  3. Add all the spices.
  4. Let the Punsch simmer for 20 minutes, so that the spices have enough time to infuse the liquid.
  5. Finally, add the alcohol and serve the Punsch in a beautiful glass or cup.
Nice! Put on your favourite Christmas playlist and enjoy a glass of Punsch!
